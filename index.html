How ADDUS Works

An intelligent, automated workflow that takes you from high-level objectives to finalized creative assets.

01

Define Your Goal

Simply input your core objective. Whether it's to increase resort bookings, launch a new product, establish a brand identity, or run a lead gen campaign.

> Input: "Launch high conversion campaign for boutique eco resort in Goa."
02

AI Business Understanding

Our core LLM agent reads and learns about your company's values, tone of voice, visual aesthetics, and existing content architecture.

> Processing branding assets... Tone: Eco luxury, Minimal. Primary color: Earth green.
03

Creative Strategy

ADDUS crawls market trends, competitor output, and target demographics to recommend custom campaigns and strategic directions.

> Strategy generated: 4 cinematic shorts, 3 brand identity style sheets, 1 site redesign.
04

Intelligent Proposal Generation

Receive a comprehensive project structure outlining scopes, timelines, exact deliverables, and workflow dependencies automatically.

> Generated proposal #ADD 889. Time to delivery: 12 days. Revisions: Unlimited within scope.
05

Transparent Pricing

No estimation game. Our pricing engine compiles rates, resource costs, and platform fees into one transparent flat quote.

> Total Flat Cost: $3,200. Breakdown: AI tokens: $0, Strategy: $800, Creator payouts: $2,400.
06

Creator Matching

ADDUS matches strategic roles in the proposal with background-checked creative talent specializing in your industry vertical.

> Matched: Lead Designer (Elena R., 98% rating), Director of Copy (Marcus G., 100% rating).
07

Project Execution

All collaboration, file exchanges, version control updates, and client review feedback loop in a unified workspace.

> Phase 1 draft uploaded. Awaiting client review. Milestone 1 of 3 complete.
08

Automated Quality Review

AI check tools verify compliance with brand guidelines, resolution specs, format constraints, and style guides prior to final client handoff.

> Checklists passing: Color fidelity (100%), Aspect ratios (100%), Legal check (Passed).
09

Delivery

Securely access, export, and deploy your production-ready design, video, copy, and structural assets directly.

> All deliverables approved. Assets archived in Cloud Vault. Integration sync ready.
